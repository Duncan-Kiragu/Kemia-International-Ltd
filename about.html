About us

Company Overview

Meet Kemia International Ltd. We are an East African company based in Nairobi, specialising in chemicals for all walks of life – especially the so called Speciality Chemicals. Kemia International Ltd. is located within the High Chem complex on Mogadishu Road, off Lunga Lunga Road in Nairobi’s industrial area, where we have sales offices, a modern German built speciality chemicals plant and warehousing facilities.
