Chemistry at its finest

We at Kemia pride ourselves in providing the ideal chemical solutions to you. Meet Kemia International Ltd. We are an East African company based in Nairobi, specialising in chemicals for all walks of life – especially the so called Speciality Chemicals.


Kemia International Limited Covid-19 Statement

The global pandemic has had and will continue to have a devasting impact around the world. That's why we remain focused on continuing to use our size and scale, as well as our compassion and commitment to social responsibility, to support and help communities worldwide.
From our supply chain, to our factories, and right through to your homes, we are working around the clock. On the ground we are mobilizing substantial local relief efforts in the communities where we operate.
